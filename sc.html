Shedd Capital Web Design

Goal

Our goal was to design and develop a modern, responsive website for Shedd Capital that would improve their visibility while clearly communicating their values, achievements, and points of contact.

What I Did

I led both the design and development of the Shedd Capital website, with a focus on creating a clean, intuitive user experience. I facilitated collaboration between business and development team members, introducing them to the web design process and foundational UX principles I had learned in university.

Each team created a prototype in Figma, and I organized peer reviews to encourage constructive feedback and iterative improvement. We then presented our concepts to stakeholders, and the final design incorporated key elements from each proposal.

I also contributed to the early development of the site using Django, helping translate the final design into a fully functional product.

Design

Results

The final product was a fully functional website with a clean, accessible interface that effectively communicates the company’s approach, services, team, and contact information. We also integrated stakeholder provided content to ensure the site accurately reflected the company’s voice and goals.

Through this project, I strengthened my ability to design for real-world clients, balancing aesthetic decisions with usability and business needs. I also gained experience leading interdisciplinary collaboration, guiding teams through an iterative design process, and delivering a product from concept to deployment.
